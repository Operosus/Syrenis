Europe: Pioneering Comprehensive Digital Governance

The EU continues to set global standards with groundbreaking regulations. DORA, the Data Act, and the AI Act build upon GDPR's foundation, creating a robust framework for the digital economy.

EU AI Act: A Risk-Based Approach

The world's first comprehensive AI law categorizes systems by risk, with stringent rules for high-risk applications and outright bans for unacceptable uses. This tiered approach is set to become a global model for AI governance.
